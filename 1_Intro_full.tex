The theory of shadowing of approximate trajectories (pseudotrajectories)
of dynamical systems is now a well-developed part of the global theory of
dynamical systems (see, for example, the monographs \cite{PILSDS, PALSDS}).
This theory is closely related to the classical theory of structural stability.

It is well known that a diffeomorphism has various shadowing properties in a neighbourhood of a hyperbolic set 
%[3, 4] 
and a structurally stable diffeomorphism has various shadowing properties on the whole manifold %[5, 6, 7].
